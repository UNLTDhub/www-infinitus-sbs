How It Works

The Adoption Process

We take adoption seriously — not to make it hard, but to make sure every match is right for both you and the animal.

1

Browse & Choose

Browse our available animals online or visit one of our shelters in person to meet them.

→
2

Submit Application

Complete our adoption application form. We'll review it and follow up within 2 business days.

→
3

Meet & Greet

Schedule a meet and greet at our shelter to spend time with the animal and see if it's a fit.

→
4

Welcome Home

Once approved, complete the adoption agreement and bring your new family member home!

Not Ready to Adopt? Foster Instead.

Fostering provides a temporary home while an animal waits for adoption — and it saves lives. No long-term commitment required.
